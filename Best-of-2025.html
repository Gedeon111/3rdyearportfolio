Entry #6: Best of 2025

Music, moments, and memories

Best of 2025

Exploring Pitchfork's top albums of the year and reflecting on my own highlights.

Pitchfork's Best 50 Albums of 2025
Q1

Have you heard any albums from Pitchfork's Best Albums of 2025 list? What did you think about them?

I've heard of many albums that released this year, like from Bad Bunny, PinkPantheress, Alex G and a couple of other artists. They surely weren't their best albums, but they were quite nice.

Q2

Which 2025 albums from the list did you listen to, and which one did you like the most? Why?

I've listened to "MUSIC" by Playboi Carti, "Fanc That" by PinkPantheress and "DeBÍ TiRAR MáS FOToS" by Bad Bunny. I have to be honest, I really had to listen to MUSIC many times, until I finally was able to like it. I only liked a couple of songs on the other albums, some were good some were bad.

Q3

Are there any albums from 2025 NOT on this list which you think should be? Which ones, and why?

I think the Album from Drake and PARTYNEXTDOOR "$ome $exy $ongs 4 U" shouldve definitely been in the Top 50 somewhere. It's the same scenario with MUSIC that I needed multiple sessions until I liked it, but after that I really enjoyed every song from the album. The album was really booming on social media, I really heard every song every day on TikTok or Instagram.

Q4

Aside from music, what were some of the BEST things of 2025 for you? Make a list of 3-5 things.

NO GLAZE, but I think one of the best things in 2025 were the english classes, because they were always so chill and I really enjoyed the time I could spend with my friends and also learn something. I also loved summer vacation, even though I was in Albania again, like the other 15 years, I still enjoyed it more the other times. It felt like I was more concious about everything and I just love to do absolutely nothing and just chill the whole day at the beach or on the balcony. I also loved the China trip I had. I just loved it living like a millionaire and exploring such a different culture.
