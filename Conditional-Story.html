Entry #4: Conditional Story

Two timelines, one decision

⑂

The Fork in the Road

Every choice creates a new timeline. This is the story of how one decision shaped everything — my friendships, my knowledge, and who I am today.

Starting an Apprenticeship at a Company Partnered with ZLI

The choice that would determine which version of my life I would live.

What Actually Happened

The Path I Took

I started my apprenticeship at a company that sent me to ZLI in my first year. From day one, I was surrounded by people who shared my interests and ambitions in IT.

The connections I made there weren't just classmates — they became close friends. We learned together, struggled through projects together, and celebrated our successes together.

ZLI gave me more than just technical knowledge. It gave me a community, a support system, and friendships that continue to this day.

✓ Built deep, lasting friendships with like-minded people
✓ Gained extensive IT knowledge and practical skills
✓ Found a supportive community that pushed me to grow
The Alternate Timeline

If I Hadn't Gone to ZLI...

If I had started an apprenticeship where I wasn't sent to ZLI in the first year, my life would look completely different today.

I probably would have ended up in a different friend group — people I might barely talk to now. Without ZLI, those deep connections would never have formed.

My IT knowledge would be nowhere near what it is today. ZLI challenged me, pushed me, and gave me the foundation that everything else is built on.

✗ Would have missed out on close friendships
✗ Would lack the depth of IT knowledge I have now
✗ Might be in a friend group I barely connect with

Looking Back

Sometimes the biggest changes in our lives come from decisions we didn't even know were important at the time. Choosing that apprenticeship wasn't just about a job — it was about finding my people and becoming who I am. I'm grateful for the timeline I'm in.
